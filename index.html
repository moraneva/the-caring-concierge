The Caring Concierge

About Me

Simply stated - I LOVE people! The Caring Concierge was conceived to assist families and their loved ones with non-medical caring assistance. So many folks find themselves hanging on to their last bit of independence - staying in their own home. Independence is maintained, but isolation is also very real. Let me help. I bring a fresh perspective to what can often times be daunting for families. Today's world makes it hard to focus on a full time career as well as nurturing and providing companionship for our elder loved ones. Call me today to meet and discuss how I can help you and/or your loved one.

Services

Companionship Visit

Do you have a senior parent who would benefit from having a lunch or coffee companion? We can dine at home or at a local restaurant. Food and companionship go hand in hand!

Appointment Transport and Accompaniment

Medical or personal appointment with the inability to drive? No time to take your parent or spouse to his/her appointment? Let The Caring Concierge handle the transportation and accompaniment. Note taking also available.

Meal Prep/Laundry

Often times the most miniscule tasks can be the most daunting. Let me assist with preparing the favorite meal or snack and handle the laundry too.

Chronicle personal history for family

This touches home for me as I join millions of other folks who say, "I wish I would have asked my Mom/Dad _________" Allow your loved one to share their history to pass along for many generations. What are their earliest memories of life? What family history knowledge do they want to share? What events most impacted their lives? I will chronicle via voice and/or written recording.

Companionship Walking

Allow me to assist your loved one on a walk, ensuring that they are safe and getting the exercise they need.

Airport Transportation

Pick up and drop off at requested airport and accompany to security check in if requested.

Social Outings

Friendships are treasured! Let me assist in facilitating visits to/from friend's home, luncheons, etc. Also happy to accompany and transport to social outings such as a trip to the DIA, bingo, etc if client is able.

Shopping

Is your loved one unable to get out and shop on their own? If they are able to safely travel and navigate the mall, the grocery store, or Costco, I will accompany and assist with a shopping adventure.

Bill Paying

I'll assist with bill organization and paying, saving time and postage.

Correspondence assistance

While sending cards and letters seems to be a lost art today, I'm available to handle the correspondence to your loved ones via letters and cards.
