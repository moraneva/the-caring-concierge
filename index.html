About Me

Simply stated - I LOVE people! The Caring Concierge was conceived to assist families and their loved ones with non-medical caring assistance. So many folks find themselves hanging on to their last bit of independence - staying in their own home. Independence is maintained, but isolation is also very real. Let me help. I bring a fresh perspective to what can often times be daunting for families. Today's world makes it hard to focus on a full time career as well as nurturing and providing companionship for our elder loved ones. Call me today to meet and discuss how I can help you and/or your loved one.

COVID 19 Updates

As our lives continue to be effected by the Covid 19 Pandemic, The Caring Concierge has taken the following steps to provide care and socialization for our experienced and treasured population:

  • Having a Covid test on myself every 2 weeks
  • Seeing folks with either an N95 mask (if required) or disposable or cloth mask
  • Gloved (when requested or required)
  • Clothed with disposable apron (when requested or required)
  • Liability Insurance policy updated and current
  • Recently underwent TB testing
  • Can provide Michigan State of Police background check

While the pandemic forced me out of work for a number of months, I'm prepared to assist you and your loved ones with needs while maintaining as safe as an environment as possible. The social isolation that has been forced on each and every one of us, has really wreaked havoc, especially with those folks suffering any level of dementia. Please reach out today with any questions, and to schedule a masked and distanced meeting.
